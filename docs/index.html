Everything it does

The whole feature set

SlyOS is a lot of apps collapsed into one agent. Here's the full breakdown.

The agent

⌨️
One-prompt home

Type or speak "what should happen?" — it understands and runs the right action(s) in order.

🎙️
Voice & tap-to-talk

Hold to speak; replies can be read back aloud with on-device text-to-speech.

🚀
App autocomplete

Start typing an app name and open it instantly, no full agent call.

📷
Camera vision Q&A

Point the camera and ask — it answers, and remembers what you looked at.

The memory brain

🧠
Imports your history

WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram, Telegram exports — tens of thousands of messages, searchable.

🕸️
Living synapse graph

People, topics, papers and notes as a pan/zoom brain that lights up the path behind each answer.

🔎
Ask your memory

"What did Papa say about the trip?" "Do I know any VCs?" — answered from everything you've fed it.

🌱
Grows on its own

Auto-saves facts it learns in conversation and re-learns your writing voice as you go.

📥
Everything feeds it

Live messages, sent texts, emails, calendar, checklist, papers and published posts all flow in.

👁️
Total Recall (opt-in)

An on-device service reads on-screen text into private memory — passwords never captured.

Messaging & outreach

💬
Replies in your voice

Full thread context + learned style across WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, Signal, Instagram and more.

🎭
Per-platform personas

Professional on LinkedIn, funny on Instagram — a tone you set per app.

🤖
Autonomous mode

Optional auto-reply with an undo window and a night schedule — covers every app with a reply box.

✈️
Telegram bot brain

Runs your Telegram bot: reads images & PDFs, answers, and replies with no notification limits.

🔗
LinkedIn networking

Reaches your whole network, drafts warm openers, and proposes who to reconnect with weekly.

📅
Books your calls

Shares your Calendly when there's genuine interest, and answers availability from your calendar.

Social post composer

Photos → an on-brand caption with a themed LinkedIn/Instagram preview, editable by prompt.

🌶️
Spicy takes for X/Reddit

Get it

Install in 3 steps

No computer needed on your end — just the file and your own key.

↓ Download SlyOS.apk

Android 10+ · ~50 MB

On your Android phone

  1. Tap Download, then open the file. Allow "install from unknown apps" once, then Install.
  2. Open SlyOS → the setup wizard takes your Anthropic key, then optional profile & imports.
  3. Press Home → SlyOS → Always. It's now your home screen.
🔑 You bring your own Anthropic API key.

Get one free at console.anthropic.com → API Keys → Create Key (starts with sk-ant-). Usage runs on your own account, and SlyOS stores the key only on your phone — never shared.
