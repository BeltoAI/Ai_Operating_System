New — the agent loop

Now it acts, not just answers

SlyOS doesn't just reply — it thinks in steps: search the live web, recall your brain, find a contact, then do the thing. Everything it does for you is visible and reversible.

Live web, out loud

Real answers, no browser toss

Ask “what’s the weather in LA” or “who won last night” by voice or text — it searches the web and answers you directly. Chains steps too: “find Anna’s email and send her the invite.” Free on Gemini’s grounded search.

Aggressive, but safe

Everything it sends, in one place

A “Sent for you” timeline logs every autonomous reply — what, to whom, and why — each with one-tap Recall. Unknown senders are drafted, never auto-sent. Trusted people get full auto.

It gets ahead of you

Proactive, not just reactive

A flight or booking confirmation lands as a one-tap “add to calendar + remind me to leave.” Overnight it quietly distills your chats into durable facts, so recall gets sharper, not noisier.

Hold the brain to talk to your agent — hands-free, with web + memory + actions. Free uses your phone’s voice; bring your own ElevenLabs key to hear it in a cloned voice.

Floating brain

Read any screen, anywhere

A glassy SlyOS bar floats over every app. Tap the brain and it reads what's on screen, explains it, and remembers it — a chat, a webpage, a form, anything. Swipe it away when you're done.

Camera & OCR

Scan receipts, docs & faces

Point the camera: a receipt logs the expense with line items, any form / ID / invoice is auto-filed into the right folder, and it recognizes the people you've taught it — front or back camera.

Inbox that files itself

Gmail auto-ingest

Get it

Install in 3 steps

No computer needed on your end — just the file and your own key.

↓ Download SlyOS.apk

Android 10+ · ~50 MB

macOS · Linux · Windows · iPhone  coming soon

On your Android phone

  1. Tap Download, then open the file. Allow "install from unknown apps" once, then Install.
  2. Open SlyOS → pick your model (Claude, GPT, or free Gemini) and paste a key, then optional profile & imports.
  3. Press Home → SlyOS → Always. It's now your home screen.

Full setup & permissions guide, step by step →

🔑 Bring your own model key — and start free.

SlyOS runs on Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini. Gemini has a free tier (aistudio.google.com), so you can run the whole thing at $0 — or paste a Claude/OpenAI key for heavier work. Add more than one and SlyOS orchestrates them (cheap model for replies, powerful one for papers) and predicts your monthly cost. Keys are stored only on your phone — never shared.
