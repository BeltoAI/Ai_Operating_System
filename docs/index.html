Get it

Install in 3 steps

No computer needed on your end — just the file and your own key.

↓ Download SlyOS.apk

Android 10+ · ~50 MB

macOS · Linux · Windows · iPhone  coming soon

On your Android phone

  1. Tap Download, then open the file. Allow "install from unknown apps" once, then Install.
  2. Open SlyOS → pick your model (Claude, GPT, or free Gemini) and paste a key, then optional profile & imports.
  3. Press Home → SlyOS → Always. It's now your home screen.
🔑 Bring your own model key — and start free.

SlyOS runs on Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini. Gemini has a free tier (aistudio.google.com), so you can run the whole thing at $0 — or paste a Claude/OpenAI key for heavier work. Add more than one and SlyOS orchestrates them (cheap model for replies, powerful one for papers) and predicts your monthly cost. Keys are stored only on your phone — never shared.

Make it yours

Feed it your history

Optional, but it's what makes the brain actually you. Export your chats from each app, then load them in the setup wizard — or anytime under Brain → About (chats) and Now → Reconnect (LinkedIn).

WhatsApp

Open a chat → ⋮ menu → More → Export chat → Without media. Share the .txt to your phone. Repeat per chat.

LinkedIn

linkedin.com → Settings → Data privacy → Get a copy of your data → tick Connections & Messages. They email a .zip.

Instagram

App or web → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions → Download your information → choose Messages, format JSON.

Telegram

Telegram Desktop → Settings → Advanced → Export Telegram data → format Machine-readable JSON.

Messenger / Facebook

facebook.com → Settings → Your information → Download your information → Messages, format JSON.

SMS & the rest

Incoming texts and any app with a reply box are learned live as you use SlyOS — no export needed.

Drop the exported files on your phone (the requested .zip/.txt/.json), then pick them in setup → "Bring in your data". SlyOS detects each format, imports the messages, and learns your writing voice from them.
