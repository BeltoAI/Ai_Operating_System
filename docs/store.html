Three kinds of power

Skills, apps & tools.

Every power is one of three shapes — decided by where its intelligence lives. The store handles the wiring for each; you just tap add.

Skill

A prompt/workflow pack that upgrades the AI itself. Added straight into the brain — no server, instant.

e.g. Deep Research, Taste, Huashu Design

Connect

A self-hosted app the AI reads & writes over an endpoint you connect (or a hosted one).

e.g. Perplexica, Immich, AppFlowy, n8n, Twenty

Tool

A file or media transform the AI can run — OCR a scan, cut a background, clone a voice.

e.g. OpenVoice, OCRmyPDF, rembg, Spleeter
